The "Visual Riddle" Protocol

How do we prove you own the item without showing you the photo? We use Generative AI to create a "Visual Riddle."

1
Image Extraction
The Finder uploads a real photo of the item (e.g., a Black Watch).
2
AI Hallucination
Our backend (SDXL) generates 5 hyper-realistic "fake" versions of that watch and 1 AI-reconstruction of the real one.
3
The Selection Test
To claim the item, the user must pick the correct watch from a grid of 6 AI images. Only the true owner knows the specific scratches, dial color, and strap details.
